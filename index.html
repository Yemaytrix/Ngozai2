About Ngozai

Founded by Microsoft technology pioneers and co-creators of the first Power Automate and Power Virtual Agent templates.

Ngozai was born from a simple observation: organizations were investing heavily in Microsoft Copilot but getting poor results because their SharePoint data was a mess. We saw enterprises spending $30/user/month on Copilot licenses while users struggled with irrelevant responses and couldn't find the information they needed.

With deep expertise across the Microsoft ecosystem—Power Platform, SharePoint, Copilot Studio, and AI Builder—we built a solution that prepares SharePoint environments for AI success through automated data organization and cleanup.

Beyond our core SharePoint business, we're also pioneering AI solutions in healthcare, including a breast cancer clinical trial screening agent that addresses the enrollment crisis affecting cancer research.
